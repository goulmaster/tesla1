Tesla model S

The Tesla Model S is designed from scratch as an electric car. The body, chassis, restraints and battery placement technology provide unsurpassed protection for the driver and passengers, minimizing the likelihood of overturning the car. Each Model S includes the latest Tesla active safety features, such as automatic emergency braking.

The Tesla Model S has the lowest coefficient of aerodynamic drag and unsurpassed efficiency. Advanced aerodynamic elements combined with a wider body and chassis allow you to overcome any road sections with incredible speed and maneuverability.

The Tesla Model S is designed to provide the most comfortable interior - an updated second row with additional leg and head space and a retractable armrest, accommodates up to three adults. The central 17-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 2200 x 1300. The restyled steering wheel is a steering wheel without levers and gearshift. USB-C wireless fast charging for each passenger and Bluetooth for multiple devices. 960W audio system with 22 speakers and active noise reduction. Powerful and unobtrusive ventilation of the cabin. Three-zone temperature control, ventilated front seats and HEPA air filters are included as standard. The front and rear trunk, as well as the ability to fold the rear row of seats, allow you to place all the necessary luggage in the Model S.

With the help of the autopilot system, your car can turn, accelerate and brake independently and, thus, cope with the most stressful driving areas. With each wireless software update, the latest improvements are available instantly. The function of fully autonomous driving - Full Self-Driving expands and complements the capabilities of your car.
